Principal Software Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Solve complex architectural challenges and improve product fundamentals.
  • Drive innovation and act as a change agent.
  • Collaborate with Program Managers and Designers to define specifications.
  • Lead technical initiatives, including architecture, design, and DevOps practices.
  • Optimize and maintain code for performance and maintainability.

Required Qualifications:

  • 10+ years of experience in software development and front-end applications.
  • Strong expertise in C#/.NET and Azure cloud platform.
  • Proficiency in Power Platform technologies.
  • Experience with AI technologies like Copilot Studio.
  • Bachelor's/Master's in Computer Science or 14+ years of industry experience.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with large-scale platforms and DevOps practices.
  • Contributions to open-source projects or personal project portfolios.
  • Experience with first-party applications and AppSource deployment.
