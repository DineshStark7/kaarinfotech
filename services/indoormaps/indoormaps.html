Event Wayfinder

We all are so acquainted with the blue dot on our navigation apps. It seems like the blue dot, which travels with us as we move through the world, has become our world. But the truth is, it cannot follow us into all environments. Most of the navigation maps represent outdoor spaces. But what if we tell you that Kaar Infotech can transform the way you navigate and engage within indoor spaces, especially during events! Presenting Event WayFinder - Moving the blue dot Indoors for easy Wayfinding.

Event WayFinder in a Glimpse

Indoor Wayfinding is a powerful tool for enhancing the visitor experience by reducing confusion, improving accessibility, and ensuring efficient navigation for saving time in large indoor spaces such as shopping malls, airports, hospitals, office buildings, convention centers, museums and other large complex structures.

Floor Plan Preparation

Obtain the floor plan of the event venue, preferably in a digital format (e.g., CAD diagram).

Data Digitization

Convert the floor plan into a vector map format compatible with the wayfinding platform.

Azure Maps Integration

Integrate the digitized floor plan with Azure Maps, a geospatial service by Microsoft.

Power BI Integration

Connect the wayfinding system with Power BI for user navigation data visualization and reporting.

Interactive Indoor Mapping Solutions

Event WayFinder comes handy in 3 different avatars to cater to your likes and preferences.

Web Application

Search and navigate your way through indoors with Event WayFinder’s web application. Locate elevators, restrooms, shops, and activity locations with a click.

Mobile App

Get real-time updates & location-based reminders on your smartphone for seamless navigation.

On-site Interactive Kiosks

Touchscreen kiosks for privacy-focused navigation. Input your destination and receive step-by-step directions.

Opening up a World of Possibilities

Imagine that you are walking into the venue of a large conference event with little knowledge about the building. In such a case, the Event WayFinder app can open a world of possibilities for you. Let’s find out how.

  • Search for specific locations – registration desk, exhibits, restrooms, elevators, entrances, exits, and more.
  • Search for specific interests – session titles, room numbers, speaker names, topics, shopping sections, and more.
  • Fetch optimal routes to different spaces with accurate distances and times.
  • High-quality, multi-floor navigation with automatic and manual options.
  • Plan your schedule based on availability, occupancy, opening/closure times, Wishlist events, and more.
  • Accessibility/wheelchair routes for comfort and convenience.
  • Highest security standards to protect personal information.
  • Reduce your carbon footprint with eco-friendly travel options based on walking time and nearby places.
  • Achieve quick evacuation times and procedures in an emergency.
  • Real-time updates for route changes, closed paths, and unexpected obstacles.
