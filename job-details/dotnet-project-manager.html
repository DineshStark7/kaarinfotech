.NET Technical Project Manager

Date posted: Jun 13, 2024
Work site: Work From Home/Hybrid
Role type: .NET Technical Project Manager
Job Description

KAAR Infotech is on the lookout for a seasoned .NET Technical Project Manager to join our innovative team. This role demands a professional with a robust background in managing and delivering development projects within the Microsoft technology stack.

As a Technical Project Manager, you will have the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects, lead a talented team, and contribute to the success of our clients. If you are passionate about technology and project management, we would love to hear from you!

Responsibilities
  • Deliver development projects across the Microsoft stack.
  • Resolve problems and drive teams towards efficient project delivery.
  • Ensure project delivery and milestones are met on schedule.
  • Identify interdependencies and ambiguities within project teams.
  • Report project progress to the Delivery Manager timely.
  • Proactively identify and mitigate project risks.
  • Adapt to changes in technologies and industry standards.
  • Interact effectively with various stakeholders.
Technical Skills
  • 10-12 years of technology expertise, with at least 4 years as a technical project manager.
  • Hands-on experience developing applications in .NET and Azure.
  • Proficiency in C#, Web API, ASP.NET MVC, and ASP.NET Core.
  • Knowledge of Azure IaaS, PaaS, PowerShell, and Azure Networking.
  • Customer-facing experience with strong communication skills.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
