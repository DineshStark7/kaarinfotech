Technical Architect

Date posted: Jun 13, 2024

Job Description

KAAR Infotech, a Preferred Supplier for Microsoft, is committed to fostering a flexible work environment that boosts productivity and sparks creativity. We are excited to invite you to join our dynamic team as a Technical Architect, collaborating closely with Microsoft clients.

As a people-centric company, we prioritize our employees, maintaining a low attrition rate and offering continuous learning opportunities within our Product division. Our team of over 100 professionals, with extensive experience in Microsoft products, ensures ample opportunities for career development and professional growth.

Ready to work on high-profile Microsoft client projects and contribute to cutting-edge product development? Apply now and join us on our mission to drive innovation and excellence in the tech industry!

Responsibilities
  • Design software systems with various Microsoft technologies and ensure compliance to all architecture requirements.
  • Coordinate with various departments and analyze requirements of the architectural team.
  • Design architecture for different components and validate tools.
  • Monitor application architecture and ensure appropriate implementation of solutions.
  • Coordinate with architects and analysts and maintain technical knowledge.
Technical Skills
  • 5+ years of experience developing web-based software using ASP.NET MVC, SQL Server, and Microservices.
  • Proficiency in JavaScript, jQuery, and modern web standards.
  • Ability to produce technical specifications, designs, and architectures.
  • Development of high-quality applications with robust testing strategies.
