Introducing Badge Scanner

Our Badge Scanner is the ultimate solution for smooth and efficient event registration. Gone are the days of long lines and manual check-ins. Simply scan attendees' badges, powered by QR code technology ,
and instantly register their attendance for each session in real time. Whether you're hosting a conference, seminar, or workshop, our app makes tracking attendance a breeze.

Why choose our Badge Scanner

Speed & Efficiency

Say goodbye to slow, cumbersome check-in processes. With fast QR code scanning, your attendees get into the event quickly.

Data-Driven Decisions

With detailed attendance data at your fingertips, you can make informed decisions on session popularity, attendee engagement, and even marketing efforts for future events.

Enhanced Attendee Experience

Ensure your attendees have a seamless experience from start to finish by minimizing wait times and streamlining event logistics.

Compatibility with Event Management Tools

Our app integrates effortlessly with existing event management systems, making it easy to incorporate into your current setup.

How Badge Scanner Works

1

Set Up

Obtain and digitize venue floor plans to create an indoor navigation-friendly map.

2

Scan

During the event, staff use the app to scan attendee badges as they enter or exit sessions.

3

Track

The app automatically logs attendance for each session, providing real-time data on participation.

4

Report

After the event, view and download detailed attendance reports to gain valuable insights.

Key Features

Effortless Badge Scanning

Utilize cutting-edge QR code technology to instantly scan and register attendees as they enter or leave sessions. The app’s intuitive interface makes the process simple for event staff, ensuring no delays or confusion.

Real-Time Session Tracking

Keep track of which sessions your attendees are joining, ensuring you have accurate, up-to-date attendance data. The app updates instantly, so you can monitor participation in real time.

Customizable QR Codes

Personalize attendee badges with unique QR codes. Each attendee receives a badge that links directly to their registration details, making check-ins seamless and secure.

Automated Attendance Reports

Say goodbye to manual attendance logs! The app automatically generates reports on attendee participation, helping you analyze session popularity, and engagement, and gain insights for future events.

Secure and Reliable

Your event data is in safe hands with our secure app platform. Each scan is encrypted and logged, ensuring that all information is stored and transmitted securely.

Easy-to-Use Interface

Designed with simplicity in mind, our app is user-friendly and requires minimal training for your event staff. They’ll be scanning badges and registering attendance in no time.
